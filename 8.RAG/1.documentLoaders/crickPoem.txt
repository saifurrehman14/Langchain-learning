Under golden sun and floodlit skies,
A game of passion gently lies,
Where leather meets the willow’s grace,
And dreams are chased across the space.

The bowler runs with steady stride,
A storm of focus locked inside,
He leaps, he turns, the ball takes flight,
A blazing spark in pure delight.

The batter stands with watchful eye,
Bat raised against the azure sky,
A heartbeat still, the moment tight,
Then cracks the ball with all his might.

It races fast across the ground,
A roaring cheer becomes the sound,
The crowd erupts in waves so loud,
Emotion dancing in the crowd.

Fielders sprint with desperate pace,
Each step a test, a thrilling chase,
A dive, a slide, a fleeting chance,
In cricket’s ever-changing dance.

The umpire watches calm and wise,
With steady hands and knowing eyes,
A finger raised, a verdict clear,
A sigh, a shout, a groan, a cheer.

From dusty streets to stadium grand,
This game unites each heart and land,
A simple bat, a humble ball,
Yet holds a magic over all.

The spinner weaves a subtle spell,
Where mystery and patience dwell,
The ball it dips, it turns, it bends,
A puzzle no one comprehends.

The fast bowler like thunder roars,
Unleashing speed that spirit soars,
A bouncer flies, a yorker strikes,
A test of skill that each one likes.

Scoreboards tick with rising thrill,
Each run a climb, a test of will,
A partnership begins to grow,
Like rivers steady as they flow.

The captain plans with thoughtful mind,
Strategic moves so well designed,
A field adjusted, angles tight,
A game of chess in open sight.

The final overs, tension high,
As every ball goes flashing by,
A single run, a boundary bright,
Could change the fate this very night.

A last-ball hit, the crowd holds breath,
Between sweet victory or death,
The bat connects, the ball takes wing,
A moment fit for song to sing.

It clears the rope, a six so grand,
Joy erupts across the land,
Teammates rush in wild embrace,
Triumph shining on each face.

Yet in defeat there’s honor too,
For those who fought with courage true,
They walk away with heads held high,
Beneath the same unending sky.

For cricket is not just a game,
It’s hope, it’s pride, it’s more than fame,
A tale of grit, of rise and fall,
A timeless love that binds us all.
